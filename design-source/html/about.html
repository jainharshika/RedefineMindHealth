Meet Your Practitioner

With over a decade of clinical experience, my approach is rooted in the belief that effective therapy requires both rigorous evidence-based practices and a profoundly empathetic environment.

I specialize in working with individuals navigating complex life transitions, chronic stress, and relationship dynamics. My methodology integrates cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness and somatic awareness, tailoring each session to the unique contours of your experience.

Beyond the clinical jargon, my goal is to provide a space where you feel seen, heard, and respected—a sanctuary where the hard work of healing feels supported and attainable.
